About

Recently coming into the world of web development I bring a fresh perspective and a desire to learn and grow within the industry. Before pursuing a career in web development, I had a background in computer classes during high school. This experience helped me develop strong problem-solving techniques, and communication skills, which have proven to be valuable assets in my new developing role.

Looking ahead on this journey so far, I am very excited to continue developing new skills and collaborating with experienced teams on real-world projects. My ultimate goal is to contribute to building websites and applications that can help solve problems and make life easier for users.
